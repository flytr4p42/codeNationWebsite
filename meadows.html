Meadows in the Mountains 2019

The Rhodope Mountains in Bulgaria are home to one of my favourite festivals, such a beautiful place.

The mountain air is as clean as it comes, it really does something for your soul. With the festival spread over the tops of two peaks, you get a mega workout while you're there. I felt knackered but invigorated stomping around.

My good pals the Chicken Brothers always bring the party :)

Some of the best sunrises you'll ever see.
