Bluedot 2019

I was fortunate to be invited by Henge to come to Bluedot 2019, a last minute surprise that made me smile

Henge not only performed as a band on the main stage, next to the massive Lovell telescope, but also DJ'd for a good few hours later on. It was lot's of fun :)

I spent most of my time with the Henge guys, though I did check out the rest of the festival a little
